About TEADEF

TEADEF provides continuous and contextual anti-doping education. Our objectives are to:

  • Provide accurate, up-to-date anti-doping information (WADA/NADA updates)
  • Deliver interactive learning modules and quizzes to boost retention
  • Offer personalized learning paths using analytics
  • Support multilingual access and offline caching for low-connectivity areas
